Platform Notes

Moody is a Health & Wellbeing platform built for STEI-ITB students and their psychologists. Superadmins review registration requests and manage access. Psychologists publish wellness content and monitor patient mood trends. Patients log daily moods and interact with published resources.

Backend: Node.js + Express · Database: SQLite (better-sqlite3) · Auth: JWT · Files: Multer · External API: ZenQuotes.io
